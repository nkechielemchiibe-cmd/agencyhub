Privacy Policy

Effective date: 18 April 2026

AgencyPortal helps job seekers find verified recruitment agencies across Canada and the United Kingdom. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have — on our website and in our Android app.

Who we are

AgencyPortal ("we", "us", "our") is operated from Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. Our service is available at www.agencyportal.ca and through our Android application distributed on Google Play.

This policy applies to both the website and the Android app. Where something is specific to one or the other, we call it out.

Information we collect

Information you provide

If you contact us, register as a recruiter, send a message to a recruiter through the service, or submit a form, we receive whatever information you choose to include — typically your name, email address, login credentials (for recruiters), and the content of your message.

Information collected automatically

When you use the website or app, we automatically receive standard technical information, including:

  • Device type, operating system, and app or browser version
  • IP address and general location (country or region, not precise location)
  • Pages or screens you visit and the time of your visit
  • Crash reports and error logs, when something goes wrong

Cookies and similar technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, and understand how the site is used. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the site may not work correctly if you do.

Android app permissions

Our Android app is a wrapper around our website, built with Median. Android may request the following permissions, each for a specific reason:

Internet access

Required to load content from agencyportal.ca. Without this, the app cannot function.

Phone state (READ_PHONE_STATE)

This permission is included by the Android framework used to build the app. It is used by the app framework to determine whether a phone call is active, so audio and video playback in the app can pause appropriately. We do not read, collect, transmit, or store your phone number, device identifiers, SIM information, or call logs. No data tied to this permission ever leaves your device.

Storage and downloads

If you download a file from within the app (for example, a PDF from an agency listing), Android asks for permission to save it to your device. Files are saved only where you choose and are not uploaded anywhere by us.

How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the service — showing you agency listings, routing your messages to recruiters, maintaining recruiter accounts, and keeping the app working
  • Improve the service — fixing bugs, measuring which features are useful, and planning improvements
  • Keep the service safe — detecting abuse, spam, and security issues
  • Respond to you — answering questions or support requests you send us
  • Meet legal obligations — complying with applicable laws and lawful requests

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with advertisers. We do not use it to build advertising profiles.

How we share information

We share information only in these limited situations:

  • With recruiters you choose to contact. If you send a message to a recruitment agency through AgencyPortal, that agency receives your message and the contact details you provided.
  • With service providers. We use trusted third parties to host the service, send email, and analyse traffic. These providers process information on our behalf under contracts that require them to protect it. Current providers include Google (hosting, analytics), Cloudflare (content delivery), and Firebase (backend services).
  • For legal reasons. If we are required by law, court order, or legitimate legal process to disclose information, we will do so.
  • In a business transfer. If AgencyPortal is ever acquired or merged, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction. You will be notified before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

Data retention

We keep information only as long as we need it to provide the service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Technical logs are typically kept for no more than 90 days. Messages and account information are kept while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct information that is inaccurate or incomplete
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent, where we rely on consent to process your information
  • Lodge a complaint with your data protection authority

To request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information, email us at hello@agencyportal.ca. We will respond within 30 days.

Canadian users have rights under the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). UK users have rights under the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect the information we hold — including encryption in transit (HTTPS), access controls, and regular security reviews. No system is perfectly secure, but we take the safety of your information seriously and address issues promptly when we find them.

Children

AgencyPortal is intended for adults who are looking for work. The service is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

International transfers

AgencyPortal is operated from Canada and serves users in Canada, the United Kingdom, and elsewhere. Your information may be processed in countries other than the one you live in. When we transfer information internationally, we rely on recognised safeguards such as standard contractual clauses to protect it.

Third-party links

AgencyPortal links to agency websites and other third-party resources. We do not control those sites and are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please read their policies before sharing information with them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our service, the law, or industry practice. If we make a material change, we will post the updated policy here and update the effective date at the top. Continued use of AgencyPortal after the change takes effect means you accept the updated policy.
